**D2C Customer Churn Intelligence Project – Part 1**



**Project Overview**



This repository contains the work completed for Part 1 of the D2C Customer Churn Intelligence project. The objective of this phase was to understand the business problem, audit the available datasets, and perform exploratory data analysis before building any predictive models.



**Files Included**



\- eda\_audit.ipynb – Data audit and exploratory analysis notebook

\- data\_quality\_report.md – Summary of data-quality findings

\- business\_memo.md – Business recommendations based on the analysis

\- requirements.txt – Project dependencies



**Work Completed**



The following activities were carried out during this phase:



\- Loaded and reviewed all available datasets

\- Examined data quality issues such as missing values and duplicates

\- Performed exploratory analysis on customer behavior and activity

\- Created visualizations to identify important patterns and trends

\- Developed churn-risk hypotheses supported by analytical evidence

\- Documented business recommendations and observations



**Key Outcome**



The analysis provided a strong understanding of customer behavior and highlighted several factors that may influence churn. These findings serve as the foundation for the feature engineering and modeling work completed in later stages of the project.



**How to Run**



1\. Install the required packages listed in requirements.txt.

2\. Open the notebook file.

3\. Run the notebook cells in sequence to reproduce the analysis and visualizations.
